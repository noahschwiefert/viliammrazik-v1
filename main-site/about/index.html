The creative process behind my products is driven by necessity, research, exploration of materials and manufacturing techniques. Being exposed to traditional crafts from my home, Slovakia, inspires me to link these often-forgotten techniques with today's values, aesthetics, materials and technologies.
I am focused on creating functional wearable products that embrace a paradoxical blend of primitiveness and futurism. These ideas merge with my ever-evolving texture archive, forming the foundation of my current practice.
